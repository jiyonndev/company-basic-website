WHO WE ARE

Rivermade OPC doing business under the names of Rivermade Enterprises and Rivermade Facilities Solutions registered with Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) of the Republic of the Philippines, and a Philippine Contractors Accreditation Board (PCAB) Licensed Contractor.

Rivermade OPC is a sales, construction and technical consultancy services company managed by professionals and technical specialists with expertise in technical sales, engineering, design and construction of buildings, infrastructures and industrial facilities.

The Project Sales Engineers, skilled technicians and craftsmen of RIVERMADE have also vast experiences working on different facilities management construction projects.

Mission

To exceed customer expectations by providing safe, excellent and on time delivery of products and services.

Vision

To be recognized as a trusted Sales, construction, facilities solutions and technical consultancy company with the passion of setting high standards and getting best results in al cha lenges of a project. To protect its people by giving the importance of safety at project sites. To protect the company by attaining its financial goals, at the same time providing career development of its personnel and giving its share for the betterment of the society and country as a whole.

Values

Dedication, Commitment, Honesty and Professionalism
